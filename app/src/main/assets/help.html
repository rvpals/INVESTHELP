Top Bar & Portfolio Button

The top bar is always visible and provides quick access to your portfolio.

  • The portfolio value button shows your total portfolio value with daily change in parentheses (color-coded green/red).
  • Second row shows Day % and All-time % gain/loss.
  • Tap the button to refresh all prices from Yahoo Finance and navigate to Dashboard.
  • A spinner appears while refreshing; a status bar below shows each ticker being updated with its price and daily change.
  • The hamburger menu (three lines icon) gives access to Accounts, Watch List, Settings, SQL Explorer, Help, and About.

Dashboard

Your portfolio overview at a glance. All cards are collapsible with pin persistence.

Portfolio Summary

  • Large total portfolio value at top with today's gain/loss amount and percentage.
  • Mini line chart showing portfolio value history (tap to open full-screen Change History dialog).
  • Full-screen chart: zoomable multi-series (Total/ETF/Stock lines), weekly change summary, and data table with daily change columns.

Market Indices

  • Horizontal row of cards: NASDAQ, S&P 500, Dow, Gold, Russell 2K, Silver, Oil, Bitcoin.
  • Tap a card to open Yahoo Finance for that index.
  • Long-press and drag to reorder cards. Order is saved automatically.
  • Customize which indices appear in Settings > Preferences.

Daily Glance

  • Overall daily change for Stocks and ETFs (dollar amount and percentage).
  • Toggle "By Per Share" to switch between total value and per-share change.
  • Top 5 gainers (green) and top 5 losers (red) today.
  • Tap any row to navigate to that item's detail screen.

Positions Pie Chart

  • Visual allocation by ticker with shares labels inside slices.
  • Legend table: Ticker, Shares, Value, % columns with gridlines.
  • Top 20 shown with "More" button. Tap a row to open item detail.

Position Details

  • Horizontally scrollable table: Ticker (with icon), Shares, Price, Cost, Value, Change $, Change %.
  • Sortable column headers (tap to sort ascending/descending).
  • Tap any row to navigate to item detail.
  • Alternating row colors for readability.

Items (Positions)

Manage every investment you hold. Each item is identified by its ticker symbol (one record per ticker).

  • Supports types: Stock, ETF, Bond, Mutual Fund, Crypto, Other.
  • Sort by Ticker, Total Value, or Current Price via dropdown.
  • Brokerage-style card rows: ticker icon + ticker name, current price with day change, total value with gain/loss badge.
  • Tap Refresh All in the toolbar to fetch latest prices and company names from Yahoo Finance.
  • Tap an item row to open its detail screen.
  • Edit button on each row; Delete available inside the Edit dialog.
  • New tickers are auto-created when you record a transaction for a ticker that doesn't exist yet.
  • Company logos auto-fetched from multiple CDN sources during price refresh.

Item Detail (4 Tabs)

Detailed view with four tabs: Details, Price History, Analysis Info, Transactions.

Details Tab

  • Header card: company logo, ticker, company name, type, current price.
  • Row 1 (large): Total Shares, Total Value, Total Cost, Total G/L.
  • Row 2 (medium): Daily G/L, Daily G/L/Share, Daily Min Price, Daily Max Price.

Price History Tab

  • Timeframe selector: Hourly (today's market hours), Daily (last 60 days), Monthly (last 13 months), Yearly (last 15 years).
  • Hourly intervals: Every Hour, 30m, 15m, 5m, 1m.
  • Interactive line chart: pinch-to-zoom (1x–5x), pan when zoomed, tap to select a point (shows tooltip), double-tap to reset.
  • Summary cards: Average, Max, Min.
  • Grid table below with row numbers, date/time, and price.

Analysis Info Tab

  • Auto-fetches data from Yahoo Finance on screen load.
  • Key Metrics: Market Cap, P/E, Forward P/E, EPS, Dividend Yield.
  • Price Range: 52-Week High/Low, 50-Day Avg, 200-Day Avg.
  • Financials: Analyst Target, Revenue/Share, Profit Margins, ROE.
  • About: Business summary.
  • Tap any metric label to see its definition.

Transactions Tab

  • "Transactions & Stats" panel: date range filter, buy/sell statistics (avg/max/min price), and per-transaction gain/loss cards.
  • "Investing Performance for <TICKER>" panel: fetches Yahoo Finance prices around each transaction date.
  • Line chart shows transaction prices (red dots), market prices (gray dots), and current price (teal dot) with price labels on each point.
  • Chart supports pinch-to-zoom, pan, tap-to-select tooltip, and double-tap to reset.
  • Data table below with Date, Price, Type columns (BUY/SELL, Market, or Current).

Transactions

Record every buy and sell. Transactions are independent of accounts.

  • Fields: date, time (optional), action (Buy/Sell), ticker (editable with dropdown suggestions), shares, price per share, total amount, note.
  • Analyze Price — button next to Price field. Shows current price, your avg/max/min, and historic high/low (week/month/year/max). Tap a price to copy it back to the form.
  • View — button next to Ticker opens item detail without losing form state.
  • Simulate — calculates days since transaction date and opens simulation with that custom range.
  • Update/Create and Simulate buttons are fixed at the bottom (always visible).
  • Each transaction card shows gain/loss and days since purchase.

Multi-Select & Bulk Delete

  • Long-press a transaction to enter selection mode.
  • Checkboxes appear; use "Select All" in the top bar.
  • Tap Delete to remove selected transactions.
  • Respects the "Warn before delete" setting.

Simulation

See how an investment would have performed over time using historical Yahoo Finance data.

  • Enter a ticker and number of shares.
  • Time ranges: 1W, 2W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, 2Y, 5Y, 10Y, MAX (grouped in Week/Month/Year rows).
  • Summary card shows start price vs current, profit/loss amount and percentage.
  • Line chart with filled area and start price reference line.
  • Tap a point for a tooltip showing price and date.
  • Custom day ranges when launched from Transaction form's "Simulate" button.

Accounts

Track your brokerage accounts (for performance tracking purposes).

  • Create accounts for different brokerages, IRAs, 401k plans, etc.
  • Each account shows its last recorded value (from most recent performance record).
  • Account detail shows info card, interactive performance chart (when 2+ records), and performance records table.
  • Deleting an account cascades — removes associated performance records.
  • Note: Items and transactions are NOT tied to accounts.

Account Performance

Track account values over time with charts and records.

  • Add Record: select an account, enter total value (or tap "Pull" for app-calculated value, "Recent" for last recorded value), optional note.
  • Mini chart preview shows selected account's history.
  • Multi-account overlay chart: select accounts via FilterChip toggles; each gets a distinct color.
  • Pinch-to-zoom (1x–5x), two-finger pan, tap for tooltip (shows account name, value, date, note), double-tap to reset.
  • Double-tap the inline chart to open full-screen chart dialog with all the same interactions.
  • Data points with notes shown as bold circles; tapping shows note text in tooltip.
  • Chart Data panel: tabular view of visible chart data (Account, Date, Value).
  • Records grid: filterable by account, sortable columns, edit notes via pencil icon.
  • One record per account per date (unique constraint).

Watch List

Track tickers you're interested in buying or monitoring.

  • Create multiple named watch lists — each displayed as its own collapsible panel.
  • Add a ticker with shares count and price-when-added; tap "Fetch" to pull current price from Yahoo Finance.
  • Table columns: Ticker, Shares, Current Price, Added Price, Change $, Change %, Date, Delete, Reminder.
  • Tap a ticker name to navigate to Item Detail.
  • Rename or delete watch lists from the panel header.

Reminders

  • Set an optional reminder per watch list item (date, time, and message).
  • Bell icon in the table row — colored when a reminder is active.
  • Notifications fire at the scheduled time; tap the notification to open Item Detail for that ticker.
  • Edit or clear reminders from the reminder dialog.

Settings

Configure app behavior and manage your data.

Preferences

  • Themes: 22 selectable color themes (Default Green, Ocean Blue, Sunset Orange, and many more). Instant apply, works in light and dark mode.
  • Auto Update Change History: when enabled, automatically records daily portfolio values after price refresh.
  • Auto Refresh All: enable periodic background price refresh (intervals: 5 min, 30 min, 1 hr, 5 hr, Market close daily). Uses WorkManager with foreground notification.
  • Auto-update position shares: toggle for CSV import behavior.
  • Warn before delete: on by default; when off, skips confirmation dialogs.
  • Dashboard Market Indices: toggle which indices appear; use up/down arrows to reorder.

Data Management

  • CSV Import: 3 import types — Transaction Records, Position Details, Performance Records.
  • Each type has "Define Mapping" (full-screen mapping editor) and "Start Import" buttons.
  • Mapping editor: preview rows, auto-map with brokerage aliases, Save/Save As/Load named profiles.
  • Import shows detailed per-ticker log (NEW/UPDATED/SKIPPED with field changes).
  • Clear button per import type to erase all entries from a table.
  • Backup & Restore: export/import full database as JSON. Supports v1/v2/v3/v4 formats.

SQL Explorer

Power-user tool for direct database access.

  • Run any SQL: SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE, PRAGMA, EXPLAIN.
  • Table browser: lists all tables with expandable column details (name, type, PK/NN indicators).
  • Tap "Open" on a table to run SELECT * FROM table.
  • Result grid with horizontal/vertical gridlines and alternating row colors.
  • Tap a row to view all field values untruncated in a detail dialog.
  • CSV Export: share query results via the system share sheet.

Chart Interactions

All charts in the app share these gestures:

  • Tap a data point to select it and see a tooltip with price, date, and details.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in (up to 5x).
  • Pan (drag with two fingers) to scroll when zoomed in.
  • Double-tap to reset zoom and scroll position.
  • Tap the same point again to deselect it.

Tips & Shortcuts

  • Tap the portfolio button at the top to refresh all prices at once.
  • Company logos load automatically from the web and are cached in the database.
  • New tickers are auto-created when you add a transaction for an unknown ticker.
  • Pin dashboard cards (star icon) to keep them expanded by default.
  • Use Analyze Price before buying to see historical high/low ranges.
  • Long-press a transaction to start multi-select mode for bulk operations.
  • Long-press and drag market index cards on the dashboard to reorder them.
  • In any chart, double-tap to reset zoom to default view.
  • The Investing Performance chart shows how your purchase prices compare to market prices over time.
  • CSV import auto-detects common brokerage column names (Symbol, Description, Price, Quantity, etc.).
  • Named CSV mappings let you save and reuse column configurations across imports.
  • All tables use alternating row colors and visible gridlines for easy reading.
  • The app log (About > Show Log) captures price fetch results and errors for debugging.
  • Set up Auto Refresh in Settings to keep prices updated in the background.
